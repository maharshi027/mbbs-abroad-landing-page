Admission Process & Eligibility

Admission Steps

  1. Submit application & documents (Class 10/12, passport).
  2. Receive university offer letter.
  3. Pay registration/tuition as per invoice.
  4. Visa invitation & embassy appointment.
  5. Pre-departure briefing & travel.

Eligibility

  • Age: 17+ by 31st December of admission year.
  • Academics: 50%+ in PCB (Class 12) for General; 40% for reserved as per norms.
  • NEET: Qualifying score as per latest NMC guidelines.
  • Valid passport & medical fitness.
